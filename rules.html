Rhesmos Global Rules

Rhesmos is built around anonymous discussion and freedom of expression. To keep the platform open and enjoyable for everyone, all users are expected to follow these rules.

General Rules

  • Do not post anything that violates applicable laws.
  • You must be at least 18 years old to use Rhesmos.
  • Do not post child sexual abuse material or exploitative content of any kind.
  • Do not post personal information (doxxing), encourage harassment, or organize raids against individuals or communities.
  • No threats of violence or credible calls to commit illegal acts.
  • No spam, flooding, or automated posting.
  • Advertising, scams, referral links, begging, or unsolicited promotions are prohibited.
  • Do not impersonate moderators, administrators, or other users.
  • Do not upload malicious files, malware, or content intended to harm users.
  • Respect each board's purpose. Keep discussions relevant to the board you are posting on.
  • Avoid low-quality posts such as repeated copypastes, meaningless spam, gibberish, or excessive duplicate threads.
  • Do not abuse the reporting system by submitting false or malicious reports.
  • Do not attempt to bypass bans or moderation actions.
  • The use of bots, scraping tools, or automated posting systems is prohibited unless explicitly authorized.
  • Do not upload content that contains hidden malicious files or embedded executable data.

Content

  • Graphic, adult, or otherwise sensitive content belongs only on boards where it is explicitly permitted.
  • Clearly mark spoilers when appropriate.
  • Illegal content will be removed immediately and may be reported to the appropriate authorities.
Remember: Anonymous speech comes with responsibility. Rhesmos values open discussion, but the moderation team may remove content or suspend access to protect the platform, comply with legal obligations, or maintain a healthy community.
