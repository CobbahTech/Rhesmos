Community Rules

By entering Rhesmos, you agree to follow these rules. They exist to keep discussions open, raw, and safe for everyone.

  • 01 Follow all applicable local and international laws.
  • 02 You must be at least 18 years old to use Rhesmos.
  • 03 Do not post child sexual abuse material or any exploitative content involving minors.
  • 04 Do not post personal information (doxxing), encourage harassment, or organize raids.
  • 05 No threats of violence or promotion of illegal activities toward any person or group.
  • 06 No spam, flooding, scams, or unsolicited advertising of any kind.
  • 07 Do not impersonate moderators, administrators, or other users.
  • 08 Keep posts relevant to the board or thread you are posting on.
  • 09 Avoid low-quality spam, repeated copypasta, gibberish, or duplicate threads.
  • 10 Do not abuse the reporting system or use it to silence others.
  • 11 Do not attempt to evade bans or circumvent moderation actions.
  • 12 No bots or automated posting unless explicitly authorized by admins.
  • 13 Do not upload malware, malicious scripts, or harmful files of any kind.
