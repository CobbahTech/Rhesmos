Rhesmos Rules

By using Rhesmos, you agree to follow these community rules. They exist to keep discussions open, respectful, and enjoyable for everyone.

Global Rules

  • Follow all applicable laws.
  • You must be at least 18 years old to use Rhesmos.
  • Do not post child sexual abuse material or exploitative content.
  • Do not post personal information (doxxing) or encourage harassment or raids.
  • No threats of violence or promotion of illegal activities.
  • No spam, flooding, scams, or excessive advertising.
  • Do not impersonate moderators, administrators, or other users.
  • Keep posts relevant to the board you're posting on.
  • Avoid low-quality spam, repeated copypasta, gibberish, or duplicate threads.
  • Do not abuse the reporting system.
  • Do not attempt to evade bans or moderation actions.
  • No bots or automated posting unless explicitly authorized.
  • Do not upload malware or malicious files.

Anonymous speech comes with responsibility. Rhesmos reserves the right to remove content or suspend access when necessary to protect the community or comply with legal obligations.
